Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the application "Lucy Content Bot" ("the App"), operated by thaProduct ("we", "us"), handles information.

1. Information We Process

The App connects to social media accounts owned by the operator using official platform APIs. It processes access tokens and account identifiers solely to publish content to those accounts. The App is operated for a single operator and is not intended to collect data from third parties.

2. How We Use Information

Access tokens and account identifiers are used exclusively to authenticate with, and publish content to, the connected social media accounts. We do not sell or share this information with third parties.

3. Data Storage

Credentials are stored securely within the automation environment used to operate the App and are not exposed publicly.

4. Third-Party Services

The App interacts with third-party platforms (such as TikTok and Instagram) through their official APIs. Their handling of data is governed by their own privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

Access tokens are retained only as long as necessary to operate the App and are removed when no longer needed.

6. Your Rights

As the operator and sole user, you may revoke the App's access at any time through the connected platform's settings.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
